2 Biology, General ~612

Feeds into MSU's top-ranked public-university programs in Plant & Animal Science and Agricultural/Biological Science (US News Best Global Universities).
3 Psychology, General ~587

Other East Lansing sites

Lansing Community College – East Campus (2827 Eyde Pkwy) is a satellite instructional site, not an independent institution — its students, faculty, and housing figures roll up into Lansing Community College as a whole (main campus in downtown Lansing). LCC has no student housing at any site and is a fully commuter institution.
